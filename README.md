# Psychological Health Diagnostic Tool

## Overview

A Python-based interactive assessment application designed to evaluate psychological well-being across multiple mental health dimensions. This diagnostic tool helps users identify areas of concern and provides personalized recommendations for improving mental health.

## Features

- **10-Question Assessment**: Comprehensive evaluation covering:
  - Stress levels
  - Anxiety and worry
  - Mood and hopelessness
  - Loss of interest in activities
  - Sleep disturbances
  - Fatigue and exhaustion
  - Concentration and memory problems
  - Emotional regulation
  - Loneliness and isolation
  - Daily functioning capacity

- **1-10 Scale Scoring**: Quantifiable responses for precise measurement
- **Automated Score Calculation**: Average score computation across all dimensions
- **Result Interpretation**: Four-tier psychological health classification
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Targeted advice for areas requiring attention

## Requirements

- Python 3.x
- No external dependencies required

## Usage

```bash
python psychoanalyza.py
```

1. Read the introduction and confirm participation (enter 'a' for yes or 'n' for no)
2. Answer all 10 questions using a scale from 1-10:
   - 1 = Not at all
   - 10 = Completely
3. Receive your overall psychological health score with interpretation
4. Get specific recommendations for problem areas

## Score Interpretation

- **1-3**: Good psychological state
- **3-5**: Mild difficulties - rest and self-care recommended
- **5-7**: Elevated risk - professional consultation advised
- **7-10**: High psychological burden - strong recommendation for expert help

## Important Disclaimer

This tool is designed for **self-assessment and educational purposes only**. It is **not a substitute** for professional psychological or psychiatric evaluation, diagnosis, or treatment. 

If you experience elevated risk or high burden indicators, please consult:
- A qualified psychologist or psychiatrist
- School counselor or mental health professional
- Mental health support services in your country
- Your primary healthcare provider
